Construction Supervision — All Project Types

Our engineers provide construction supervision across buildings and infrastructure, including bridges, water tanks, retaining walls, and multi-storey developments.

  • On-site supervision and inspection of all works — formwork, rebar, concreting, steel erection
  • Monitoring of workmanship and material quality, ensuring compliance with all contract documents (BoQs, technical specifications, etc.)
  • Development of and monitoring work programmes, labour and equipment schedules for successful project completion
  • Managing construction activities and directing work to keep project within timeline and budget constraints
  • Coordination with contractors and subcontractors
  • Designed fixes for whatever site conditions may require

With our supervision, your project is safe, efficient, and built to specification.
