Who We Are

Nexxus Consultancy is a Plantation Prospect-based structural engineering firm providing independent advisory, design, and compliance services to the built environment across Guyana and the wider Caribbean.

We serve government agencies, institutional clients, commercial developers, and high-net-worth individuals who require engineering expertise of the highest standard — delivered with discretion, rigour, and strategic clarity.

Led by Stephon Watson, our practice combines deep technical knowledge with the commercial acumen to navigate complex projects from inception through to successful delivery and certification.
